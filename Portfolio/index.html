Personal Details

I am Modou Jallow, a passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer. I am an alum of Asia Pacific University of Innovation and Technology, pursed Electrical & Electronics Engineering. Self-taught programmer, A.I enthusiast and passionate problem solver.
I have recently involved in various projects of artificial intelligence, data science and cloud computing projects. Gaining hands-on experience and industrial insights through placements, competitions and hackathons.
I held various leadership positions in which my contributions were remarkable amongst which are; Co.founder and C.E.O of Gomindz inc., Institute of Engineers Malaysia APU Student Section (I.A.S.S), Executive Committee of Africa Society Asia Pacific University (A.S.A.P.U) and Head Coach of APU Football Team.
I have been tutored by top online coaches such as Kiril Eremenko at Superdatascience, Mathias Andersson at 'A Cloud Guru', Andrew Trask at OpenMined.org etc, nurturing and equiping me with the necessary skills and outcomes in the accelerating industry.
Enhancing and keep up with my journey of Data Science and Machine Learning, I followed some of the greatest and most notable researchers in the industry such as Geoffrey Hinton, Yann LaCun, Francois Chollet, Andrew Ng etc.
This has provided me with an amazing opportunity to constantly learn, engage and grow.
My greatest aspiration is using technologies to solve problems faced with humans, improve standards of living and sustainability, making the complex simple. There has never been a better time to live and solve problems.
